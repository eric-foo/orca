## Beauty Tells by Mechanism Class

### Class A — Fabricated accounts / bots  (Tier 4 alone; Tier 2 via network structure)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** Instagram & TikTok beauty-creator follower spikes; generic comment
  swarms; audience geography/language inconsistent with a US beauty creator; views/likes/followers
  moving in implausibly regular lockstep. Fake-follower/engagement **markets** exist (the *Devumi*
  FTC case is the enabling-infrastructure exemplar — no beauty client implied).
- **Clearing-check:** account-history depth; organic cross-venue discussion by the same handles;
  downstream behavior (branded search, retailer rank) not just posting.
- **Reliability:** follower/engagement audits are **Tier 4 (weak alone)** — legitimate virality,
  giveaways, and international expansion mimic it. Public creator-audit tools (HypeAuditor, Modash)
  are **screening, not proof**.
- **Evasion:** real-but-low-intent engagement; gradual purchases; demographically plausible targeting.

### Class B — Review-stuffing / fake reviews + insider / staged-independence  (Tier 1–2)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** Sephora, Ulta, and Amazon beauty reviews — velocity bursts decoupled
  from independent traffic; templated language; **a 5★ launch wall that later decays to 1★** (post-campaign *shift*, not a static bimodal shape — polarizing genuine products are bimodal without manufacturing); **shared-reviewer / co-review network
  overlap** (the strongest public-data tell — see the core design's §"What the External Research
  Adds"). **Insider / staged-independence:** employees/managers posting as ordinary customers
  (*Sunday Riley* — employees posting Sephora reviews under false identities, VPN after IP-based
  removal, down-voting negatives) and staffed "random shopper" testimonials (*Hismile* — employees
  presented as random shoppers, caught by identity matching).
- **Clearing-check:** verified-purchase share; review cadence tracking independent traffic;
  longitudinal varied reviewer histories; for insider risk, identity/relationship independence.
- **Reliability:** **Tier 1** when the evidence is a whistleblower/reimbursement/identity-match
  record (how Sunday Riley and Hismile were actually caught — *not* a star-pattern); **Tier 2** for
  the co-review network; **Tier 3–4** for burst/templating alone.
- **Evasion:** buy-then-refund verified-purchase fraud; drip-seeding; partitioning reviewer pools
  to defeat the network tell; **paid genuine-purchase reviews** (largely undetectable).

### Class C — Paid amplification / undisclosed promotion  (Tier 3; Tier 1 if the connection is provable)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** Instagram/TikTok beauty creators — affiliate links / discount codes
  without an upfront "Ad" label (*Charlotte Tilbury × Eliza Batten* — "#collab" not a clear ad
  label; *THG/The Fragrance Shop* — LTK "paid links" shown only after click-through; *BPerfect ×
  Charlotte Dawson* — ad label rendered white-on-white + a "Luxurious" beauty filter altering the
  promoted result); hundreds of paid posts with disclosures hidden below the "more" cut (*Teami*).
  **Promotion-engagement mismatch** (a creator promotes a product but engagement runs below their
  own baseline) is the in-band divergence tell.
- **Clearing-check:** **persistence after the paid push ends**; independent non-seeded costly
  behavior; search-interest lift that outlives the campaign.
- **Reliability:** disclosure-gap is **Tier 1 for "concealed commercial amplification" when the
  material connection is provable** — but it shows only that *origin was concealed*, **not** that
  demand is hollow (this is the (2)-vs-(3) line). Decay/divergence is **Tier 3**.
- **Evasion:** undisclosed paid; micro-influencer swarms; whitelisting/dark posts; **seed-then-real**
  (a paid push that ignites genuine demand — the (2)→(3) transition the strip-test catches).

### Class D — Coordinated origination laundering  (Tier 3)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** the NEWSY laundering pathway in this vertical (per the venue
  card-set's chain: **Reddit boards originate → trade press launders into the citable record → BoF/
  WWD terminate**) is exactly where a single coordinated/PR seed can be laundered into "everyone is
  saying." Watch for a claim whose root receipt traces to one origin then echoes as independent.
- **Clearing-check:** original-source mapping; varied phrasing from unrelated sources; use by the
  target audience, not just relayers.
- **Contrast (legitimate origination):** *INCIDecoder* (venue card 11) — commissioned SPF tests
  became the root receipt the whole Purito chain cited. That is **independent corroboration with a
  single origin**, NOT laundering. The discriminator is whether the origin is *coordinated/paid* vs
  *independent and costly* — the core's wind-caller-vs-laundering line, in beauty.
- **Evasion:** brief-driven creator scripts with deliberately varied wording; seeded "organic" UGC.

### Class E — Channel-stuffing: sell-in vs sell-through  (owner judgment call — not auto-capped)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** retail placement / restock at Sephora/Ulta/Target (**sell-IN**)
  without consumer **sell-THROUGH**. Beauty launches frequently lead with placement, so this is
  **genuinely ambiguous** (sell-in can precede real demand).
- **Clearing-check:** sell-through evidence — velocity, restock pressure driven by demand, reorders.
- **Reliability:** per core C2 Rule 3, this is an **owner judgment call** (integrity-artifact vs
  transient), **not** an automatic R3(a) cap.

### Class F — Dupe-wave / comparison distortion  (context-dependent)
- **Beauty surfaces / tells:** beauty's **dupe culture** makes price-driven rerouting one of the
  most legible demand signals there is (taxonomy pricing refinement) — but comparison-bait can be
  engineered. Tell: a "dupe" surge with no independent switching.
- **Clearing-check:** price-driven rerouting **with stated cause** — dupe adoption, trade-down,
  switching that is *costly behavior*.
- **Reliability:** often **transient, not manufactured** — route a real-but-decaying dupe surge to
  C3 as transient, not to a manufactured cap.

## Outside-In Capability Note (what an external reader can actually see in beauty)

Per the core design's **outside-in capability gap**, the *strongest* beauty integrity signals are
mostly **not** available to Orca from the public surface:

- **Reviewer-network graph (Tier 2, high value) is capture-gated.** It needs a large collected
  corpus of reviewer histories across many products (Sephora/Ulta/Amazon) — now scoped to a
  **within-category** ego-graph and spec'd in
  `orca/product/spines/capture/source_families/retail_pdp/retail_pdp_review_capture_spec_v0.md`
  (cross-industry out of scope; within-category overlap is noisier — it corroborates within the
  convergence stack, not a verdict alone).
- **First-party metadata is unavailable.** Bazaarvoice / PowerReviews catch fakes using IP, device
  reputation, and submission velocity — telemetry visible only to whoever runs the review pipeline,
  never on the published page. Orca cannot replicate that approach.
- **Tier-1 evidence is consumed, not manufactured.** A regulator action (FTC/ACCC/ASA) or a
  whistleblower post (e.g. the Sunday Riley Reddit post) is near-dispositive **when it already
  exists** — Orca can cite it but cannot produce it.
- **Net:** in beauty, Orca works mainly the **Tier 3–4** public tells (disclosure gaps, burst/
  templating, decay, engagement-without-conversion) **plus** the network graph where capture allows,
  and leans on **convergence** + **costly-behavior-under-the-layer** rather than any single detector.
