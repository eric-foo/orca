Finding a great dupe for Maison Francis Kurkdjian’s Baccarat Rouge 540 depends on your budget and whether you prefer an exact clone or a scent inspired by its signature sweet, woody-amber profile.

The most highly recommended, nearly identical alternatives include:
1. Direct Dupes (Exact Clones)
Dossier Ambery Saffron: Priced around $49, this is widely regarded as one of the best 1-to-1 matches, mimicking the original's airy, sweet, and woody aura perfectly. 
Dossier
 +3
Armaf Club de Nuit Untold: Available for around $40-$50, this Eau de Parfum is frequently praised by fragrance enthusiasts as borderline identical to BR540 with incredible projection. 
Lattafa Ana Abiyedh Rouge: A budget-friendly option (usually $20-$30) that gives you the luminous, cotton-candy and amber warmth of BR540, though it can have a slight sharpness upon initial spray.

Zara Red Temptation: At around $30, this remains one of the most accessible and popular high-street alternatives that flawlessly hits those saffron, jasmine, and woody amber notes.

2. Variations on the Scent Profile
Ariana Grande Cloud: Priced around $35-$45, this is a softer, sweeter, and more coconut/praline-forward take on the DNA. While not an exact clone, it shares the same woody, airy base that makes BR540 so universally loved.

Sol de Janeiro Cheirosa 68: A beautiful, floral and vanilla-forward body mist version that provides that same intoxicating jasmine and amber warmth for roughly $24.
