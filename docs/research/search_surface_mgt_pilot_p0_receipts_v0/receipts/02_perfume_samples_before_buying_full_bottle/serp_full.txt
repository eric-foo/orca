AI Overview
Testing perfume samples is the smartest way to find your signature scent without wasting money on a full bottle. It lets you experience how a fragrance develops on your skin, performs in different weather, and matches your personal style across multiple wearings. 
YouTube
·The Fragrant Lounge
 +1
There are several highly effective ways to acquire perfume samples, ranging from free in-store trials to budget-friendly online purchases:
1. In-Store Samples (Free)
Department stores and specialty beauty retailers like Sephora, Nordstrom, and Macy's are excellent places to try before you buy. 
How to do it: Approach the fragrance counter and ask a sales associate to create a sample for you. Most stores will happily make custom decants of 2-3 fragrances per visit so you can test them with your unique skin chemistry. 
Pro Tip: Never buy a fragrance immediately upon smelling it in the store. Apply it to your pulse points, leave the store, and wait a few hours to let the base notes fully develop. 
2. Retailer Discovery Sets (Best Value)
Many major retailers offer curated sets of samples that come with a voucher to redeem for a full-size bottle later. 
Fragrancelord.com
 +1
Sephora: Offers curated sets like the Sephora Favorites Samplers. These kits allow you to test multiple popular or trending scents, and the cost of the kit can usually be applied toward the purchase of your favorite full-sized bottle. 
3. Online Sample Boutiques (Best Selection)
If you are hunting for niche, artisanal, or luxury perfumes that local stores don't carry, dedicated online sample sites are your best option. 
YouTube
·Dedrick Hicks Jr
 +2
Brands to browse: Sites like Scent Split, Surrender to Chance, or LuckyScent allow you to purchase official manufacturer vials or exact 1ml - 5ml decants of rare and high-end perfumes. 
Byrdie
Direct from Brands: Many luxury and niche fragrance houses (such as Le Labo, Parfums de Marly, or Byredo) sell their own Discovery Sets directly on their websites.
4. Travel Sizes (The Middle Ground)
If you want to wear a scent for a week or two before fully committing, skip the tiny 1ml vials and opt for official Travel Sprays (usually 5ml - 10ml). These are sold directly by brands or at retailers like Sephora Travel Size Fragrance and offer the best evaluation of how long a fragrance actually lasts on your skin.
