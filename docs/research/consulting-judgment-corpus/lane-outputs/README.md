# Lane Outputs

Store raw or lightly normalized outputs from evidence-only research lanes.

Recommended naming:

- `YYYY-MM-DD_lane_01_structured_case_repositories_raw_v0.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_lane_02_strategy_firm_judgment_cases_raw_v0.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_lane_03_decision_family_search_raw_v0.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_lane_04_backtestability_evidence_screen_raw_v0.md`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_lane_05_reject_pattern_mapping_raw_v0.md`

Rules:

- Preserve source URLs and missing-field labels.
- Do not add scoring or tiering here.
- Do not convert consulting marketing claims into external outcome evidence.
- Mark missing fields as `not_found` rather than filling gaps from inference.
